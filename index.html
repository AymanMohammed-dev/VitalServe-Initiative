About Us

VitalServe Initiative is a student-led nonprofit focused on collecting and distributing medical supplies to communities in need. We aim to make healthcare access more equitable through organized, local action.

Our Mission

We work to reduce medical supply shortages by running donation drives, partnering with local groups, and ensuring supplies reach people who need them most.
