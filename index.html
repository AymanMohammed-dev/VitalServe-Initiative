About Us

VitalServe Initiative is a student-led nonprofit focused on collecting and distributing medical supplies to underserved communities. We believe access to healthcare resources should be a right, not a privilege.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce medical supply shortages by organizing donation drives, partnering with local communities, and delivering supplies where they are needed most.
